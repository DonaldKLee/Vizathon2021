The X axis (horizontal) represents the number of days a known protest has lasted in the years of the covid-19 pandemic from 2020 to 2021; the Y axis represents the number of people (in hundreds) who attended these protests in the years of the covid-19 pandemic from 2020 to 2021. The number of people who attend protests in the United States (red) is greater in one day than several days in comparison to Brazil (black) in which the number of people who attended protests that last several days than one day; this may be attributed to population sizes and political stability over covid-19 restrictions (ex: the United States experienced an administration change and with it, changes in restrictions).
